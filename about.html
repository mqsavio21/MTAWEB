A Shopping Landmark

Mal Taman Anggrek, a vibrant hub in West Jakarta, offers endless shopping, dining, and entertainment. With over 400 stores and top international brands, this seven-storey, 360,000 sqm mall is a go-to destination for trendsetters. It features attractions like an ice rink, cinema, and fitness center, plus numerous shops and eateries. The mall also hosts major events in its 20+ exhibition spaces, including a stunning 800 sqm atrium.

A Signature of Distinction

Mal Taman Anggrek is owned and managed by Indonesia's renowned Mulia Group, which brings with it more than 20 years of residential, commercial and hotel property development experience. At the heart of our retail operations are our tenants, and our dedicated management team works round the clock to ensure housekeeping, security and other business needs are always met. More than that, we are in tune with what today's consumers want and expect - and we deliver just that, through innovative marketing strategies and the constant upgrading of interiors, amenities, services and technology.
