Celebrates Kartini Day: Honoring the Spirit of Empowerment

In celebration of Kartini Day, Mall Taman Anggrek extends heartfelt wishes to all Indonesian women. This special day honors Raden Ajeng Kartini, a pioneer of women's empowerment and education in Indonesia. Her legacy continues to inspire generations of women to pursue equality, education, and independence.

As we commemorate this important day, Mall Taman Anggrek invites everyone to reflect on the spirit of Kartini and her contributions to gender equality. Let's continue to uplift and support women in all walks of life, empowering each other for a brighter future. Happy Kartini Day!
